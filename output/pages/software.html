Corax FM Software

Accounting has become very software driven over the last few years. Corax FM take a proactive look at the software landscape and will accept your accounts in any software package that you may use: Xero, Sage50, Excel, or even plain text. We have even developed some software in house.

Hermes

Hermes is your favourite grade 37 bureaucrat. It is also our very own, free cloud accounting software. Why not create an account and see what Hermes can do for you?

Cosearch

Do you follow companies on Companies House? Do you want a simpler tool to see data? Take a look at Cosearch. Cosearch was developed internally to see a summary of some of the Companies House data. It is under active development and updates are added regularly.
